Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I join a community?

Go to the Communities page and click on the "Join Now" button under the community you want to join.

How can I submit a blog post?

Once logged in, navigate to the Blogs page, and you'll find an option to submit your post.

How do I reset my password?

If you forgot your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it.
